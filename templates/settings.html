Option A: Use Existing Phantom Wallet (Recommended)

Export your private key from Phantom and use it with the bot. This allows you to keep using your existing wallet.

üì± For Phantom Browser Extension:
  1. Open Phantom extension in your browser
  2. Click your profile avatar (top left corner)
  3. Click "Manage Accounts" (pencil/edit icon)
  4. Select the account you want to export
  5. Click "Show Private Key"
  6. Enter your Phantom password to confirm
  7. Copy the private key (base58 string)
  8. Paste it in the box below
üì± For Phantom Mobile App:
  1. Open Phantom app
  2. Tap your profile avatar (top left)
  3. Tap the ‚öôÔ∏è Settings icon (top right)
  4. Scroll down and tap "Security & Privacy"
  5. Tap "Export Private Key"
  6. Select the account you want to export
  7. Authenticate with password or biometrics
  8. Copy the private key (base58 string)
  9. Paste it in the box below
Your private key will be securely saved to wallet.json locally
‚ö†Ô∏è Security Warning:
  • Never share your private key with anyone
  • Only paste it in trusted applications (like this bot running locally)
  • The key is stored locally on your computer, not sent anywhere
  • Consider using a separate wallet for trading to limit risk

üì§ How to Send SOL to Your Wallet:
A
From a Centralized Exchange (Recommended for Beginners)
  1. Log in to your exchange account (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, etc.)
  2. Navigate to your Solana (SOL) wallet
  3. Click "Withdraw" or "Send"
  4. Paste your wallet address (from Step 1 above)
  5. Enter the amount of SOL to send (recommended: at least 0.5 SOL to start)
  6. Confirm the network is "Solana" (NOT Solana-wrapped tokens)
  7. Complete the withdrawal
‚è±Ô∏è Transfer Time: Usually takes 1-5 minutes
B
From Another Solana Wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
  1. Open your existing Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
  2. Click "Send" or transfer icon
  3. Paste your bot's wallet address
  4. Enter amount (keep some SOL in your main wallet for fees)
  5. Confirm and send
C
Buy SOL Directly (if you don't have any)
  1. Use services like Moonpay, Ramp, or Transak
  2. Enter your wallet address as the recipient
  3. Follow their KYC process
  4. Purchase SOL with credit/debit card
‚ö†Ô∏è On-ramp services may have fees and require identity verification
üí∞ Recommended Starting Amounts:
  • Testing: 0.1 - 0.5 SOL (~$10-50)
  • Small Scale: 0.5 - 2 SOL (~$50-200)
  • Medium Scale: 2 - 10 SOL (~$200-1000)

Remember: Only trade with money you can afford to lose!
